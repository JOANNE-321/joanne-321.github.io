DISCRETE STRUCTURE

It was sometimes difficult but also quite interesting to learn about Discrete Structures, which include functions, set theory, counting, probability, graph theory, and finite automata. It made it easier for me to understand the fundamentals of how computers manipulate and arrange data. I discovered how to use probability and counting to make wise decisions. Finite automata taught me that even complex objects had basic elements, and graph theory illustrated the connections between various components of computers. It was fantastic to solve problems and see how everything comes together in real life.
